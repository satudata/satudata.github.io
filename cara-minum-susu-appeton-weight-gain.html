Cara :

Mimi = 3/8 liter

Dodot = 2/4 liter

Samakan penyebutnya

= 3/8, 2/4

= 3/8, 4/8

3/8

= 0,375

2/4

= 0,5

disamakan penyebutnya !

3/8 = 3/8

2/(4 × 2) = 4/8
