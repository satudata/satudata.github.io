15. sebuah perusahaan makanan memproduksi dengan biaya variabel,sebesar RP.2000 /bungkus dan biaya tetap sebesar Rp.60.000.000 jika penjualan sosis sebesar Rp.20.000 bungkus.tentukan harga pokok produksi sosis tersebut​


Harga pokok produksi sosis tersebut adalah Rp 5.000 per bungkus.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui biaya variabel (variabel cost/VC)  Rp 2000 per bungkus

Biaya tetap (fixed cost/FC)  Rp 60.000.000

Penjualan sosis sebesar (Total Sell/TS) 20.000 bungkus

Biaya Produksi (total cost/TC) = Total FC + Total VC

TC = Rp 60.000.000 + ( Rp 2000 x 20.000)

TC = Rp 60.000.000 + Rp 40.000.000

TC = Rp 100.000.000

Sehingga total biaya produksi untuk 20.000 bungkus sosis sebesar Rp 100.000.000

Maka dapat ditemukan:

Harga Pokok Produksi per bungkus= TC : TS

Harga Pokok Produksi per bungkus = 100.000.000 : 20.000

Harga Pokok Produksi per bungkus = Rp 5.000

Pertanyaan di atas mungkin bukan penjualan sosis sebesar Rp 20.000 bungkus, namun jumlah penjualan sebesar 20.000 bungkus.

Rumus ini termasuk dalam hitungan Break Even Poin (BEP). Dengan mengetahui harga pokok produksi setiap barang, penjual bisa menentukan harga jual dan jumlah keuntungan yang ingin tercapai.

Jika sudah mengetahui harga jual setiap produk, maka kita dapat menentukan pada jumlah produksi minimum berapa posisi perusahaan tidak akan mengalami kerugian tapi setidaknya sudah menyentuh BEP.

Posisi BEP adalah posisi di mana perusahaan tidak mengalami kerugian namun belum mendapatkan keuntungan. Setelah mengetahui posisi BEP ini, perusahaan bisa menentukan keuntungan dari harga produksi supaya perusahaan mendapatkan laba secara total.

How to Make Really Tasty and Tasty Sausage

evi anggraini

Jul 29, 2017 · 2 min read

Before turning to a variety of recipes and how to make grilled sausages, you should know first, how do we make delicious grilled sausages. Apparently, delicious grilled sausages were not determined solely from seasoning alone. However, starting from the selection, preparation, application of sauce, to how to burn need to be considered very carefully.

You need to know that making sausage meat is not difficult at all! The key is in the things I mentioned above earlier.

If everything is done correctly, then you can guarantee that the sausage you produce will be a soft and delicious sausage and have a delicious flavor.

Well, below I will tell you a little recipe so that the sausage that you make can make the people who tasted so you want to collect and want to try it many times

Material used

5 sausages (whatever taste you want)

margarine to taste

just enough skewers

sweet soy sauce to taste

just enough tomato sauce

just enough chili sauce

Easy way to make grilled sausages

First, make a spread first by mixing sweet soy sauce, tomato sauce and chili sauce in a container.

Then stir until thoroughly mixed evenly.

Next take the sausages that have been provided then open the plastic wrapper one by one.

Then the sausage stick that has been opened with a skewer.

After that, cover it with butter until everything is completely smeared.

And burn in hot coals or a quality sausage grill.

Next, apply sausages that have been made until everything is covered.

Bake it again on the sausage grill while turning it upside down so it doesn't burn and change color to brown and perfectly cooked.

If it feels ripe, lift and serve.
