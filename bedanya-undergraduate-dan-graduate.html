3. jelaskan pengertian graduate,under graduate dan post graduate


An undergraduate is a person who has not yet taken a first degree

A graduate is a person who has completed their course and training, especially when they are awarded for their undergraduate courses

A post graduate is a person who has already received a first degree in the university and is taking a more advanced degree.

This sentence means that I didn't graduate from the University of Yew York last year (D)

Pembahasan

We use would rather or ’d rather to talk about preferring one thing to another. Would rather has two different constructions. When we want to refer to the past we use would rather + have + -ed form. So, the different construction uses past simple clause.
