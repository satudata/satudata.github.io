Captain America: Civil War is a Marvel production film released in 2016. The film is the third film of the Captain America series, after Captain America: First Avenger (2011) and Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014). The film also became the first film in the third phase of Marvel's Cinematic Universe (MCU), which also involves other superhero movies such as The Avengers, Iron-Man, Thor, The Incredible Hulk, Ant-Man and Guardian of the Galaxy. Captain America: Civil War directed by Joe Russo and Anthony Russo, and starred in a row of top actors like Chris Evans, Robert Downey Jr., Scarlett Johansson, Anthony Mackie, Elizabeth Olsen, Sebastian Stan and Jeremy Renner.

Captain America: The Civil War became one of the most important films in the series of Marvel's Cinematic Universe, where in this film the heroes of The Avengers will be divided into two camps, the Captain America camp and the Iron-Man camp. It would be interesting to see the storyline offered by Marvel, which will be a lot of emerging new characters, including the Spider-Man character played by Tom Holland and Black Panther characters played by Chadwick Boseman

Captain America Film Synopsis: Civil War (2016)

The movie "Captain America: Civil War" will continue the ending of the Avengers: Age of Ultron, as Steve Rogers takes over leading the new Avengers team, his quest to keep mankind with the Avengers team.
After some time after the international incident involving the Avengers team and causing a lot of destruction, there is a political pressure that requires superheroes to install a system of accountability and the body that determines when the team is needed.
With such a setus may make it difficult for the Avengers who are trying to protect the world, and to create a peaceful world.
On the other side Tony Stark (Iron Man) will fight a decision taken by Steve Rogers or Captain America (Chris Evans). The resistance was caused because of the enmity and the Law that was given to Superheroes for people with super abilities to reveal their identity to the US government.
That's why both of them are in disputes, because Stark supports this government program, whereas Rogers does not approve of it for threatening the hero superhero's freedom.

22. skipper means captain​


Jawaban:

The word “skipper” originated from the Dutch word schipper, which means “a person who commands a ship” . A schipper leads his crew to their destination, or achieve some other goal. In sport, the captain of a team has the same job to fulfill. Hence, he is called “skipper”

Jawaban:

General Knowledge

A skipper is known as a captain of a boat or a ship.
