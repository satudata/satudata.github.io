My Favorite Acoustic Guitar


When I was in Junior High School, my mother bought me an acoustic guitar. It was my first guitar. I learned some basic guitar chord with it. It accompanied me since I was a beginner who know nothing about guitar, until I managed to improve my skill to a better level. Even though I have another guitar right now, but this one is still my favorite. The sound produced by it is still as clear as it was. its classic and simple design is just irreplaceable.

My favorite acoustic guitar is mainly made of mahogany wood. It is a steel-string type of acoustic guitar. Three different colour were used to cover the major parts of the guitar. The colour of the front side is peach with wooden fiber pattern which intentionally designed to be clearly visible with our eye. The colour of the back side is black, and the colour of the edges is dark brown. The neck of the guitar was painted in black, but the headstock is dark brown. The tuning pegs or tuning keys are made of white plastic and is attached to the headstock, three on the top side and three on the bottom side. There is a part called "nut" placed between the neck and the headstock. It is made of white plastic too. The fret of the guitar is mainly made of copper. There is only one sound hole on my guitar and it is located at the end part of the neck. The bridge, where the end part of the strings were attached, is made of wood and is painted in black.

13. deskripsi benda mobil dalam bahasa inggris


Descriptive Text adalah kalimat yang menceritakantentang seseorang atau sesuatu. Tujuan dari kalimat itu adalah menggambarkan orang, tempat, atau benda tertentu.

Descriptive Text memiliki 2 struktur utama, yaitu

- Identification, identifikasi suatu fenomena yang akan dijelaskan

- Description, menggambarkan fenomena tersebut dalam bagian bagian dan atau karakteristik.


Dengan mengetahui penjelasan Descriptive Text, maka dapat dibuat contoh Descriptive Text sebagai berikut


I have a sedan. It has white color with a yellow trim on its door. The size is quite compact for everyday use. It has a turbo engine so the accelaration is quite high.

17. deskripsi tentang benda dalam bahasa inggris


Contoh teks deskripsi (description text) tentang benda:

I have a beautiful dollhouse. The dollhouse is very special for me because my father made it. The dollhouse is big, it is a two-story house. It has two bedrooms, a bathroom, a living room, a dining room, and also a kitchen. The roof is painted purple and the wall is pink, those are my favorite colors. You should come to my house so we can play together!

Penjelasan:

Terjemahan dari teks diatas:

Aku memiliki sebuah rumah boneka yang cantik. Rumah boneka itu sangat spesial untuk ku karena ayah ku yang membuatnya. Rumah boneka itu besar, itu sebuah rumah dua tingkat. Rumah itu memiliki dua kamar tidur, ruang keluarga, ruang makan, dapur, dan kamar mandi. Atap nya berwarna ungu dan tembok nya berwarna merah muda, itu adalah warna kesukaan saya. Kamu harus datang ke rumah saya sehingga kita bisa bermain bersama!

Teks diatas merupakan descriptive text. Descriptive text merupakan sebuah jenis teks yang bertujuan menjelaskan, menggambarkan, atau mendeskripsikan sesuatu berupa hewan, benda, lokasi, dan sebagainya. Descriptive text dibuat tanpa melakukan riset, penulis hanya perlu menuliskan cerita berdasarkan apa yang ia lihat atau ia dengarkan.

Generic structure dari descriptive text yakni:

Identification, pendahuluan berupa gambaran umum tentang suatu topik. Description, berisi penggambaran sebuah benda yang dideskripsikan seperti sifat dan tampilan fisik yang kemudian dituliskan dengan spesifik.

26. Deskripsi benda sapu dalam bahasa inggris


Writing Descriptive Text

Broom

Brooms are tools used to sweep dirt off floors. Brooms usually made out of wood, straw and twine. But nowadays, there are plastic brooms as well. Even though sometimes they are less sturdy than the wood ones, but their bristles are stronger and don’t break as often as straw bristles.

Brooms consisted of four main parts. These are brush, brush cap, the shaft and handle.

A broom is useful to sweep up various types of dry messes, such as broken glasses, dirt, etc. Usually, a sweeper holds broom’s handle in one hand, and shaft in the other hand, and swings the arms opposite direction. There are probably other techniques to sweep as well. The final result is generally to brush the dirt away with the broom’s bristles.

Pembahasan

Deskripsi tentang sapu di atas masuk dalam kategori descriptive text. Descriptive text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu tempat, orang, binatang, dll. Pada descriptive text sifatnya lebih mengarah kepada penggambaran, pengungkapan secara detil dan penjelasan.

Generic structure dari descriptive text adalah:

Identification => Pendahuluan, yakni gambaran umum suatu topikDescription => Ciri-ciri tentang topik yang dibahas, sifat-sifat yang dimiliki, tampilan khas, dll.
