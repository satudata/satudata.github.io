22. Buatkan dialog bahasa inggris tentang membatalkan pesanan tiket pesawat.


Pembahasan

Dialog Bahasa Inggris tentang membatalkan pesanan tiket pesawat adalah

Rimania: "Good afternoon. August Tour and Travel. Rimania is speaking. How can I help  you?"

Herdian: "Good afternoon. I made a reservation earlier this week, but I have to cancel it."

Rimania: "I see, may I know your reservation number, Sir?  

Herdian: "Of course. The reservation number is ID182J."  

Rimania: "I have your flight information here, Mr. Herdian Pratama. It is a morning flight from Jakarta to Sidney on January 1st, seat numbers 27A, am I right?"

Herdian: "Yes. That’s the one. Can you cancel it? Something urgent has just come up."

Rimania: "Sure, I can. Because you cancel your flight, you are eligible for 80% refund."

Herdian: "Okay. I understand."

Rimania: "Let me proceed and now your flight is cancelled."

Herdian: "Thank you very much."

Rimania: "You are welcome. Can I help you with anything else?"

Herdian: "No, that's all. Thank you."

Rimania: "You are welcome, Sir. Have a nice day."

Herdian: "You too."
