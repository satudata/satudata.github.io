7. Tulislah arti kata" berikut dan buatkan kalimat dengen benar.a. thus b. for this reasonc. consequentlyd. as a result​


Jawaban:

a) "thus" means as a result of something / consequences.

e.g. -> I failed the class and thus I have to join summer school.

b) "for this reason" means a reason / explaination for something that happens(?) idk bruv

e.g. -> It is partly for this reason that he's refusing to rush any recruitment moves.

c) "consequently" means as a result. xD

e.g. -> She was an intelligent student and consequently did well at school.

d) "as a result" means the cause and effect of something.

e.g. -> Several houses are damaged as a result of the earthquake.

9. 3. Many customers complained; as a result, the company changed its policy,The underlined clause shows...A. CauseC. EffectB. ComplaintD. Advicekalimat yg bergaris bawah dari as a result, the company changed its policy​


Jawaban:

Many customers complained; as the result, the company changed its policy

= Banyak pelanggan komplain/mengeluh; dan/dari hasilnya, perusahaan mengubah kebijakannya.

3. The underlined clause shows...

= Klausa yang digarisbawahi menunjukkan..

A. Cause ❌

= Sebab ❌

B. Complaint ❌

= Komplain/keluhan ❌

C. Effect ✔

= Efek/dampak/akibat ✔

D. Advice ❌

= Nasihat (semacam saran) ❌

Penjelasan:

Pertanyaan ini adalah sebuah pertanyaan sebab-akibat. Sebuah pernyataan dari kalimat yang tertulis didalamnya sebuah "sebab" dan juga "akibat" dari sebab tersebut.

Kalimat ini dibagi dua, yaitu sebab (many customers complained) dan satu lagi akibat (the company changed its policy), dan juga satu kata penyambung (as result).

Jadi intinya, para pelanggan Complaint (keluhan) adalah si Cause (sebab) dan sebagai Advice (nasihat/saran)nya adalah sebagai pengganti sebab yaitu si Effect (efek) atau bermaksud si perusahaannya mengganti kebijakannya.

Jawaban:

C. Effect

Berdasarkan arti:

Many customers complained; AS A RESULT, the company changed its policy,

= Banyak pelanggan mengeluh; Hasilnya perusahaan mengubah keaamanan.

The underlined clause shows...

= Kata bergarisbawah menunjukkan..

A. Cause= Sebab

C. Effect= akibat ✅

B. Complaint= keluhan

D. Advice= saran

Alasan memilih C:

Karena AS A RESULT adalah berupa akibat dari pelanggan yg mengeluh

Jadi:

Cause: Many customers complained;

Sebab: Banyak pelanggan mengeluh;

Effect: AS A RESULT, the company changed its policy,

Akibat: Hasilnya perusahaan mengubah keaamanan.
