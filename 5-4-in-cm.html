23. 3/4 of Jevan’s height is equal to 2/5 of Nicslie’s height. The difference in their height is 77 cm . What is Nicslie’s height in cm?


Jawaban:

jevan height = x

Nicslie height = y

3/4x = 2/5y

x + 77 = y

use substitution method

¾x = ⅖ (x + 77)

¾x = ⅖x + ¹⁵⁴/5

¾x - ⅖x = ¹⁵⁴/5

¹⁵/20x - ⁸/20x = ¹⁵⁴/5

⁷/20x = ¹⁵⁴/5

x = ¹⁵⁴/5 ÷ ⁷/20

x = ¹⁵⁴/5 x 20/7

x = ⁴⁴⁰/5

x = 88cm

Jevan's height is 88cm

¾x = ⅖y

¾ • 88 = ⅖y

66 = ⅖y

y = 66 ÷ ⅖

y = 66 x ⁵/2

y = 165

thus Nicslie's height is 165cm

25. In the figure, PV = VU = 3 cm, RS = UT = 5 cm and TS = 4 cm. ∠QPV = ∠PVU = ∠UTS = ∠RST = 90°. Find the area of the figure.​

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

So, we're dealing with 3 areas here. :D

Area of the figure = area of PQUV + area of QUR + area of RSTU

A = area 1 + area 2 + area 3

A = (3×3) + (3×4)/2 + (5×4) = 9 + 6 + 20 = 35 cm²
