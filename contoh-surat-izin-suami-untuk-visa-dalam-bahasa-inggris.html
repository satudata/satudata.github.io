11. Buatlah contoh surat izin cuti kuliah karena balik ke negara asal dalam bahasa inggris


Berikut adalah contoh surat izin cuti kuliah karena balik ke negara asal dalam bahasa Inggris:

Dear [Your Name]

I am writing to request a leave of absence from my studies at [Name of University] for the upcoming semester. I will be returning to my home country of [Name of Country] to attend to some urgent family matters.

I anticipate that I will need to be away from campus for approximately [Number of Weeks/Months]. My planned departure date is on [Date], and I expect to return on [Date].

During my absence, I will be staying with my family in [City, Country], and I will not have access to reliable internet or other resources required for my coursework. Therefore, I will not be able to complete any academic work during my absence.

I understand that my request for a leave of absence may affect my academic progress, but I am committed to completing my studies as soon as possible after my return. I will do everything I can to minimize any disruption to my studies, and I am willing to make up for any missed coursework or exams upon my return.

Thank you for your understanding in this matter. Please let me know if there are any additional steps I need to take to complete this request.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
