3. A car left town A at 8.45 am and arrived at town B at 11.50 am. Find the timetaken by the car for the whole journey and the distance between town A andtown B, given that the speed of the car was 84km/h Pls help


Jawaban:

The time taken

= 11.50 - 8.45

= 3.05

= 3 hours 5 minutes

= 3 1/12 hour

= 37/12 hour

The distance

= speed × time

= 84km/hour × 37/12 hour

=259km

10. Town A to Town B is 50 km away From town A, I drove the car around 100km/hr to town B What is the time did i go?


Jawab:

half an hour (1/2 hour)

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

If the distance is 50 km and the speed is 100 km/h, then:

time = distance / speed

time = 50 / 100

time = 1/2 hour

So, the time we need to drive to Town B is half an hour.

Kata sifat yang bertujuan untuk membandingkan.

Jika kata sifat terdiri dari satu suku kata, hanya perlu ditambahkan -er di akhir kata tersebut. Contohnya "strong" -> "stronger"

Untuk kata sifat dua suku kata yang tidak berakhiran -y dan untuk semua kata sifat tiga suku kata atau lebih, gunakan bentuk “more + adjective ”. Contohnya "expensive" -> "more expensive"
