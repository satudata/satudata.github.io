Simple Past Tense:

Kalimat Verbal Positif:

(+) Subject + verb 2 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(+) We attended the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Negatif:

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) We did not attend the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Interogatif:

(?) Did + subject + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Did we attend the festival last week?

13. Bentuk verbal dari past continous tense


s + was/were + v. 1 + ing + cinuous Tense


Past continuous tense berasal dari kata "past" yang berarti lampau, "continuous" yang berarti masih berlanjut dan "tense" yang berarti masa/ waktu. Jadi past continuous tense secara keseluruhan bisa diartikan sebagai kata kerja yang menerangkan sebuah action ataupun peristiwa yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau. Sedang berlangsung maksudnya yaitu action tersebut telah dilakukan namun belum berakhir. Tenses ini cocok untuk menulis short story, novel, sejarah, cerita legenda, buku diary dan peristiwa-peristiwa yang kita alami sebelumnya.

Contoh:
Fani was sleeping when Vika came ( Fani sedang tidur ketika Vika datang)Kalimat di atas menerangkan bahwa pada saatt Vika datang, saat itu juga Fani sedang tidur, masih memejamkan mata di tempat tidur, belum bangun. Ini adalah bentuk past dari present continuous tense, bedanya jika present continuous sedang dilakukan saat ini, detik ini juga, maka past continuous sedang dilakukan saat itu, detik itu juga di masa lampau.

Rumus Past Continuous Tense


Rumus tenses ini hampir sama dengan present continuous tense, yang membedakan hanya to be / kata kerja bantu saja, sementara yang lainnya tetap sama persis.

Ada dua (2) rumus, yaitu rumus verbal dan nominal.

Rumus Verbal


Berikut rumus, keterangan dan contoh kalimat verbal:
Positive: S+ to be (was,were)+ (V1+ing)+ O/AdvNegative: S+ to be (was,were)+ not+ (V1+ing)+ O/AdvInterrogative-Positive: To be (was,were)+ S+ (V1+ing)+ O/AdvInterrogative-Negative: To be (was,were)+ not+ S+ (V1+ing)+ O/AdvKeterangan:
"was" untuk subjek I dan subjek 3rd singular (she,he,it,Ayu,Zaenal,etc)."were" untuk subjek you (kamu) dan subjek plural (they,we,people,you (kalian),2 cats,etc).
a. Contoh positive:
I was reading a novel in the library (Saya sedang membaca sebuah novel di perpus)Ayu was watching a video with Tini (Ayu sedang menonton sebuah video dengan Tini)They were still playing pingpong (Mereka masih bermain pingpong)b. Contoh negative:
I wasn't reading a novel (Saya tidak sedang membaca novel)Ayu wasn't watching a video (Ayu tidak sedang menonton video)They weren't playing ping-pong (Mereka tidak sedang bermain pingpong)c. Contoh interrogative-positive:
Was I reading a novel in the library? (Apakah saya sedang membaca sebuah novel di perpus?)Was Ayu watching a video with Tini? (Apakah Ayu sedang menonton sebuah video dengan Tini?)Were they still playing ping-pong? (Apakah mereka masih bermain pingpong?)d. Contoh interrogative-negative:
Wasn't I reading a novel in the library? (Tidakkah saya sedang membaca sebuah novel di perpus?)Wasn't Ayu watching a video with Tini? (Tidakkah Ayu sedang menonton sebuah video dengan Tini?)Weren't they still playing ping-pong? (Tidakkah mereka masih bermain pingpong?)

14. rumus past perfect tense kalimat verbal dan nominal


verbal past perfect : (+) had + v3. (-) had not + v3. (?) had + s + v3? nominal past perfect : (+) had been + noun. (-) had not been + noun. (?) had + s + been + noun?Kalimat verbal:
1. Kalimat aktif:
    Kalimat positif: Subjek + Had + Kata Kerja III + Komplemen
    Kalimat negatif: Subjek + Had + Not + Kata Kerja III + Komplemen
    Kalimat interogatif: Had + Subjek + Kata Kerja III + Komplemen
2. Kalimat pasif:
    Kalimat positif: Subjek + Had + Kata Kerja III + By + Objek 
    Kalimat negatif: Subjek + Had + Not + Kata Kerja III + By + Objek
    Kalimat Interogatif: Had + Subjek + Kata Kerja III + By + Objek

Kalimat nominal:
    Kalimat positif: Subjek + Had + To Be III (Been) + Bukan Kata Kerja
    Kalimat negatif: Subjek + Had + Not + To Be III (Been) + Bukan Kata Kerja
    Kalimat interogatif: Had + Subjek + To Be III (Been) + Bukan Kata Kerja

(+) You studied English yesterday (-) You did not study English yesterday (?) Did you study English yesterday?

2. Nominal Sentence

➡ Kalimat yg TIDAK ada KATA KERJA

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + Complement (-) S + Tobe (was/were) + NOT + Complement (?) Tobe (was/were) + S + Complement?

Contoh kalimatnya:

(+) You were a student last year (-) You were not a student last year (?) Were you a student last year?
