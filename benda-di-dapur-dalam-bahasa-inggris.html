17. Sebutkan 10 benda di dapur serta kegunaannya dalam bahasa inggris


Sebutkan 10 benda di dapur serta kegunaannya dalam bahasa inggris!

There are many things that we can find in the kitchen, here are 10 kitchen utensils and kitchen equipment:

Knives: knives are used in food preparation, for cutting, slicing and chopping things like vegetables, meat and bread. Knives come in many different shape and sharpness.Cutting boards: cutting boards are used in food preparation to put on the material for cuttingScissors: Scissors in the kitchen also use mainly for cutting things, cutting open food packaging, snipping herbs and also for cutting bread. Pot and pans are cooking ware used for cooking, serving and also for storing food, such as soup, pasta and etcMeasuring Cups and Spoon: Use in food preparation to measure the ingredients such as flour, sugar and etc.Colander: It is a cooking utensil used for rinse vegetables, fruits and also to strain food such as pasta, noodle and rice.Spoon, Whisk, Spatula, tongs : these cooking utensils are used for food preparation, to lift and flip food during cooking.Microwave: microwave is used for cooking and heating food. Kettle: we use kettle to boil some waterRice Cooker: We use rice cooker mainly to cook some rice, but rice cooker also useful to cook other things too like soup, porridge and bread.
