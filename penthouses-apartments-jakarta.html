11. What is the difference between apartments and flats


Jawaban:

Generally speaking, there is no difference between an apartment and a flat. Both terms refer to a personal residence consisting of a series of rooms located on the same floor, within a larger, singular building. ... On the rare occasion the American flat is used, it is often in reference to a luxury apartment.

23. 1. Do you find any apartment in your towns or cities?2. Where are apartments usually found?3. What do apartments generally look like?4. How are apartments different from houses?5. Can you think of the advantages or disadvantagesof living in an apartment compared to living ina house?​


Jawaban:

1. Yes, I do. I find apartments in my town or city.

2. The apartments are usually found near the school or university or office area in the center of the city.

3. The apartments generally look like a big tall modern buildings with basement car park.

4. The houses have garden and spacious room (horizontal house), while apartments are limited rooms and it shapes a building. (vertical houses)  

5. The advantages of living in apartments are 24 hours security officer, a lot of facilities, cheaper than buying a house, and a privacy guarantee (free neighborhood drama). The disadvantages of living in apartments are no backyard or garden, limited spaces, signal problem, and difficult access.

Penjelasan:

1. Apakah kamu menemukan apartemen di kotamu? Ya. Aku menemukan apartemen di kotaku.

2. Dimanakah biasanya apartemen ditemukan? Apartemen biasanya ditemukan di dekat area sekolah atau kampus atau perkantoran di tengah kota.

3. Seperti apakah bentuk apartemen? Apartemen biasanya sebuah gedung yang besar, tinggi, dan modern dengan tempat parkir mobil di ruang bawah tanah.

4. Apa berbedaan apartemen dan rumah? Rumah memiliki taman dan ruangan yang luas (rumah mendatar), sementara apartemen memiliki ruangan yang terbatas dan berbentuk gedung (rumah tegak lurus).

5. Apa keuntungan dan kekurangan tinggal di apartemen dibandingkan dengan tinggal di rumah? Keuntungan tinggal di apartemen adalah keamanan terjaga 24 jam, banyak fasilitas, lebih murah daripada membeli rumah, dan kebebasan pribadi terjamin (bebas dari konflik tetangga). Kekurangan tinggal di apartemen adalah tidak punya taman atau taman belakang, ruangan terbatas, masalah sinyal, dan akses yang sulit.
