15. Bahasa Inggrisnya apakah kamu belum tidur


The translated English version of 'apakah kamu belum tidur?' is

'are you still awake?'

Such question is normally questioned by parents to their children or between lovers at night to check on them.

Penjelasan:

In translating sentences or expression, one has to consider several stuffs to make a proper one and not an ambiguous one, which normally comes up as a result of the literal or word-by-word translation. Those stuffs are

the proper vocabularythe context

In terms of context, it is quite general and not hard to understand. As for the vocabulary, there are a number of possible answers. They are

are you still awake?have you been asleep yet?are you still not sleeping yet?

However, the word-by-word translation can lead to a confusion. Here's the example:

'are you not yet sleeping?'

Such sentences is not grammatically correct.

PEMBAHASAN

Sleep: tidur.

Sleepy: mengantuk.

To sleep away: ketiduran.

---

Sleep (artinya: tidur) adalah golongan kata kerja, yang termasuk kelompok the irregular verbs (kata kerja yang tidak beraturan) sehingga perubahannya ke dalam verb-2 maupun verb-3 tidak mengikuti rumus apapun.

THE IRREGULAR VERBS "SLEEP"

Verb-1 (Infinitive): SleepVerb-2 (Past): SleptVerb-3 (Participle): SleptMeaning: Tidur.
