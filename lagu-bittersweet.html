Teks How to Make Chocolate Dipped Strawberries To make chocolate dipped strawberries, first, prepare all the following ingredients: - 2 chopped squares semisweet or bittersweet chocolate 2 tablespoon whipping cream Dash almond extract -8 strawberries Second, combine the chocolate and the whipping cream in a glass measuring cup or bowl. Microwave at medium power for 1 minute until the chocolate melts, stirring after 30 seconds. Stir in the almond extract and cool slightly. Finally, dip each strawberry into the melted chocolate, allowing the excess to drip off. Place on a waxed paper lined baking sheet. Refrigerate or freeze for approximately 15 minutes until the chocolate is set. Soal : 1. What is the purpose of the text? 2. Explain the generic structure of the text!
