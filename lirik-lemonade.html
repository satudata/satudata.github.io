Lemonade were left = 2/5 x 870 ml

Lemonade were left = 348 ml

Pembahsan

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

Present Tense adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menunjukkan suatu Kegiatan yg dilakukan secara berulang/kebiasaan dan utk menyatakan kebenaran umum.

Rumus:

1. Verbal Sentence: kalimat yg ada kata kerja

(+) S + Verb 1 (-s/es) + O

(-) S + do/does + Not + V1 + O

(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O?

2. Nominal Sentence: kalimat yg tidak ada kata kerja

(+) S + ToBe (is/am/are) + O

(-) S + ToBe (is/am/are) + not + O

(?) ToBe (is/am/are) + S + O?

Kesimpulan:

• Ada kata kerja/verb (-s/es) = verb+s (likes)

• Maka Rumus yg digunakan adalah Verbal Sentence

(+) S + Verb 1 (-s/es) + O

= he + likes + to drink a glass of lemonade coffee

25. Ruth had 6 liters 450 milliliters of lemonade. She poured an equal amount of lemonade into 5 bottles. How much lemonade did Ruth pour into each bottle?​


Jawab:Ruth had a total of 6 liters 450 milliliters of lemonade. She poured an equal amount of lemonade into 5 bottles. To find out how much lemonade Ruth poured into each bottle, we need to divide the total amount of lemonade by the number of bottles.

First, we need to convert 6 liters 450 milliliters to milliliters. There are 1000 milliliters in 1 liter, so we can convert 6 liters to milliliters by multiplying 6 by 1000, which gives us 6000 milliliters. Then, we add 450 milliliters to get a total of 6450 milliliters.

To find out how much lemonade Ruth poured into each bottle, we divide the total amount of lemonade by the number of bottles:

6450 milliliters ÷ 5 = 1290 milliliters per bottle

Therefore, Ruth poured 1290 milliliters of lemonade into each bottle.

Simple Syrup

1 cup (225 grams) sugar

1 cup (225 milliliters) water

Lemonade

1 cup (225 milliliters) lemon juice (about 6 – 8 lemons)

4 cups (900 milliliters) water

how to make:
1.find a large pitcher
2.Squeeze some lemons to make 1 cup (225 milliliters) of lemon juice
3.pour the lemon juice into the pitcher
4.Place a saucepan on the stove
5.Pour some water and sugar into the saucepan
6.Heat the sugar and water
7.Pour the sugar-water into the pitcher
8.Add 4 cups (900 milliliters) of cold water to the pitcher
9.Stir the lemonade and taste
10.Put the lemonade in the refrigerator
11.serve the lemonade
