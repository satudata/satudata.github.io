19. In a basekatball free-throw shooting contest, the shot made by Sam and Will were in Ratio 7:9. Will made 6 more shot than Sam. Find the number of shots made by each of them. Bisa di terjemahin kok kalau kagak ngerti :)


Jawaban: Sam made 21 shots and Will made 27 shots.

Penjelasan serta cara:

#We will use x as a varible to the number of shots

Sam = 7x

Will = 9x

Number of shots Will made subtracted by the number of Sam made = 9x-7x = 2x

Will made 6 more shots than Sam.

Will made 6 more shots than Sam.Will made 2x more shots than Sam.

# From the statement above, we know that 2x=6

2x = 6

x = 6÷2

x = 3

# Now that we know the value of x, we can use substitution method

Sam = 7x

Will = 9x

Sam = 7(3) = 21

Will = 9(3) = 27

30. Two elements X (atomic mass 16) and Y (atomic mass 14) combine to form compounds A, B and C. The ratio of different masses of Y which combines with fixed mass of X in A, B and C is 1 : 3 : 5. If 32 parts by mass of X combine with 84 parts by mass of Y in B, then in C, 16 parts by mass of X will combine with …a. 14 parts by mass of Yb. 42 parts by mass of Yc. 70 parts by mass of Yd. 82 parts by mass of Y


Matter : Stoichiometry
Sub matter : Dalton's Law

Ratio mass of X and Y
X ÷ Y = 16 ÷ 14
= 8 ÷ 7

Mass of X constant, forming 3 compounds of XY with ratio as follows:

A ----> Ya = 1
B ----> Yb = 3
C ----> Yc = 5

In Dalton's law, make one elements at same weight.
In this case mass of X is same/constant.


Compound A
X ÷ Y = 32 ÷ mass Ya
8 ÷ 7 = 32 ÷ mass Ya

Mass of Ya = 28

Compound B
X ÷ Y = 32 ÷ Yb
8 ÷ 7 = 32 ÷ 3Ya

Yb = 84 ----> appropiate with data known (84 parts)

Compound C
X ÷ Y = 16 ÷ Yc
= 32 ÷ 2 Yc
= 32 ÷ 2 (5× Ya)
= 32 ÷ 2 (5×28)
= 32 ÷ 140

Then if 16 parts of mass X will combine with 70 part by mass of Y

Answer is C.
