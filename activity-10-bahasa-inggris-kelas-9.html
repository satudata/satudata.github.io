1. bahasa inggris kelas 9 activity 10 ​


1. Our television is broken

=> False. Karena yang rusak hanya kulkas si penulis saja. Tidak disebutkan kalau TV nya juga rusak.

2. We went to a traditional market to buy a new one.

=> False. Karena di paragraf 1, kalimat 2; Then, my father asked me to go with him to the kitchen store to buy a new one. Jadi yang benar dia diajak ayahnya untuk membeli kulkas yang baru di kitchen store bukan traditional market.

3. There, I saw a mixer and a blender in the electronic corner.

=> False. Karena si penulis tidak hanya melihat mixer dan sebuah blender saja, tetapi ada banyak di paragraf 2, kalimat 2.

4. I also saw eggs, rice, flour, and other cooking ingredients.

=> False. Karena si penulis pergi ke toko peralatan dapur bukan toko bahan. Bisa dibaca di paragraf 2.

5. We bought a new refrigerator.

=> False. Karena melihat harga kulkas barunya, si penulis dan ayahnya tidak jadi membeli kulkas baru.

Jawaban:

1. The text is aimed at guiding the readers on how to make pepes ikan.

3. The fish are marinated with salt, pepper, lime juice and yellow paste.

4. We cook the dish by charcoal or sear in a frying pan.

5. We can eat the dish with steamed rice and vegetable.

Penjelasan:

Teks prosedur yang berhubungan dengan pertanyaan di atas adalah

PEPES IKAN

(Grilled marinated fish wrapped in Banana leaf)

1. Cut fish fillet into cubes.

2. Season fish fillet with salt, pepper, lime juice and yellow paste.

3. Place the marinated fish cubes on the banana leaf with sliced tomato, lemon basil, salam leaf, and kefir lime leaf on top. Fold into a parcel, securing the ends with toothpicks.

4. Grill over charcoal or sear in a frying pan for about 10 minutes on each side, until the leaf edges are getting charred.

5. Serve with steamed rice and vegetable.

23. activity 6 lks bahasa inggris kelas 9 halaman 78


Bahasa Inggris adalah salah satu bahasa yang bersifat universal dan berlaku di seluruh dunia sebagai bahasa internasional. Berdasarkan soal, dapat disimpulkan bahwa:

My friend and I are riding a bicycle arround the village now.We actually are living in the city at present.She always obeys the rules in our school.However, she often has a desire to do something out the box.Right now, we are enjoying the green views.Alika wears a shirt inscribed with "I'm bold" on the back side.Everything is going so far.And I hope she isn't do something that I don't expect this time.After cycling we intend to buy fresh fruit.We rarely go to a country side.

Pembahasan:

Setiap bahasa memiliki kekhasan masing-masing. Bahasa Inggris adalah salah satu bahasa yang bersifat universal dan berlaku di seluruh dunia sebagai bahasa internasional. Berdasarkan soal, dapat disimpulkan bahwa:
