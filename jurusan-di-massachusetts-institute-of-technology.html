10. if your father asks you to continue to study at tokyo institute of technology........ you be readyfor that?


Jawaban:

The answer for this sentence is

'If your father asks you to continue your study at Tokyo Institute of Technology, will you be ready for that? (it's a conditional type 1)

Penjelasan:

Conditional type 1 is used to state the possibility in the future. The pattern is

If + present simple, S + will + V1

The sentence above is definitely using conditional type 1 because it uses present simple in the main clause.

'If your father asks you to continue your study at Tokyo Institute of Technology,......'

So, it is clear enough to say that the missing word should be related to it, which is 'will'. (....will you be ready for that?'

11. Advantage and disadvantage of technology​


Advantages and Disadvantages of Technology

What is technology? Technology usually refers to the tools and machines that are developed to solve real-world problems. Today we use technology for almost everything, from mundane things like cooking to complex problems like outer space missions. However, like everything else, there are advantages and disadvantages to technology.

One evident advantage of technology is in the medical field. Medical technology has help treat sick people, save lives, and fight viruses and bacteria. The invention of the telephone, the computer, and later, the internet is also an important plus of technology. It enhances communications. Now we can easily communicate and do business with people from all corners of the world. Technology in industry makes productions faster. Workers can produce more goods in less time.

On the other hand, technology also has its drawbacks. Man becomes depending too much on technology. For example the calculator makes students lazy and not exercising their memory. This decreases the brainpower; makes people less smart. Modern weapons cause deaths and suffering of millions of people. Vehicles use fuel that harms the environment. The internet is rampant with hoaxes and propaganda that may lead to riots and anarchy.

In conclusion, while technology is indeed important and beneficial, we should also be wary of its bad aspects. Technology can improve our life but it also can ruin it. We must use it wisely.

--------------

Pembahasan

Advantages and disadvantages essay adalah salah satu essay yang cukup sering diminta untuk dibuat oleh siswa. Dalam membuat essay ini, siswa diminta untuk memaparkan keuntungan dan kerugian suatu item atau topik. Siswa juga dapat diminta memberi opini atau solusi. Essay ini biasanya bersifat akademis maka gunakanlah bahasa yang formal.

Advantages and disadvantages essay dapat terdiri dari 4 paragraf, yaitu:

✒Paragraf 1  ⇰ Pengenalan topik yang akan dibahas

✒Paragraf 2 ⇰ Keuntungan dari topik, berikan detail dan contoh.

✒Paragraf 3 ⇰ Kerugian dari topik, beri detail dan contoh.

✒Paragraf 4 ⇰ kesimpulan

Beberapa kata-kata yang dapat digunakan selain advantages and disadvantages:

14. Answer these question! 1. what is report technology text 2. what is the generic structure of report technology text 3. what is the purpose of report technology text 4. what is language feature of report technology text?


1). A report text is a type of text that announce the result of an investigation or announce something. The information given in a report text is very general information. Report presents information about something, as it is. It is as a result of systematic observation and analyses.

2). Generic Structure

Text organization of report are as follows:

a). General classification (introduces the topic of the report/tells

what phenomenon under discussion is.)

b). Description (tell the details of topic such as physical appearance, parts, qualities, habits/behavior).

3). The purpose of report text is to describe about a person, thing or place in generally.

4). Language Features

- Use of general nouns ( Whales, Kangaroo, Computer)

- Use of present tense (Komodo dragons usually weigh more than 160 kg)

- Use of behavioral verbs (Snakes often sunbathe in the sun)

- Use of technical terms ( Water contains oxygen and hydrogen)

- Use of relating verbs (is, are, has)
