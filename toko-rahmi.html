9/4+25/10=90+100/40=190/40=4,75

[tex]2 \times \frac{1}{4} + 2 \times \frac{5}{10} \\ (2 + 2) + ( \frac{1}{4} + \frac{5}{10} ) \\ 4 + ( \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{2} ) \\ 4 + ( \frac{1 + 2}{4} ) \\ 4 + \frac{3}{4} \\ 4 \frac{3}{4} [/tex]
