7. If a sector of a circle has an arc lenght of 2π inches and an area of 6π square inches, what is the radius of the circle?mohon bantuannya kak​


terjemahan: jika juring dari sebuah lingkaran memiliki panjang busur 2π inci dan luasnya adalah 6π inci², berapa radius (jari²) dari lingkaran tsb?

jawab:

luas juring (sector's area) = 6π

panjang busur (arc length) = 2π

[tex] \frac{ \alpha }{360°} \times \pi \times 2r = 2\pi \\ \frac{ \alpha }{360°} \times 2\pi r = 2\pi \\ \frac{ \alpha }{360°} \times r = 1 \\ r = \frac{1 \times 360°}{ \alpha } = \frac{360}{ \alpha }[/tex]

[tex] \frac{ \alpha }{360°} \times \pi \times {r}^{2} = 6\pi \\ \frac{ \alpha }{360°} \times {r}^{2} = 6 \\ {r}^{2} = \frac{6 \times 360°}{ \alpha } = \frac{720}{ \alpha } [/tex]

[tex] \frac{ {r}^{2} }{r} = r \\ \frac{ \frac{720}{ \alpha }}{ \frac{360}{ \alpha } } = r \\ \frac{720}{ \alpha } \times \frac{ \alpha }{360°} = r \\ 2 = r \\ r = 2[/tex]

Catatan:

[tex] \frac{ \alpha }{360°} \times \pi \times 2r \: adalah \: rumus \: menentukan \: panjang \: busur \\ \frac{ \alpha }{360°} \times \pi \times {r}^{2} \: adalah \: rumus \: menentukan \: luas \: juring[/tex]


8. Spiral Review James is building a rectangular fire pit. He dug a hole 20 inches by 20 inches. He decides to make each side of the fire pit 4 inches longer instead. What is the percent increase in the area of the fire pit?


Jawaban:

The original area of the fire pit is the product of its length and width:

Area = 20 inches x 20 inches = 400 square inches

After James increases each side of the fire pit by 4 inches, the new length and width become 20 + 4 = 24 inches. Therefore, the new area is:

New Area = 24 inches x 24 inches = 576 square inches

To find the percent increase in area, we need to calculate the difference between the new area and the original area, divide by the original area, and then multiply by 100 to convert the answer to a percentage:

Percent Increase = [(New Area - Original Area) / Original Area] x 100%

Substituting the values we have, we get:

Percent Increase = [(576 - 400) / 400] x 100%

Percent Increase = (176 / 400) x 100%

Percent Increase = 0.44 x 100%

Percent Increase = 44%

Therefore, the percent increase in the area of the fire pit is 44%.beri bintangnya kakak

Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given that a box has:

-- Length (a) = 16"

-- Breadth (b) = 10"

-- Height (c) = 5"

Find the surface area!

Surface area equation (box) =

2×(a×(b+c)+(b×c)) =

2×(16×(10+5)+(10×5)) =

2×((16×15)+50) =

2×(240+50) =

2×290 =

580 square inches

580 square inches of paper is needed to wrap the box
