(2/3 jati) + (9/3 jati) = 33.000

1 jati = 9.000
1 meranti = 1/3 jati
1 meranti = 1/3 x 9000
1 meranti = 3.000

=3x

=1,95m

x=1,95m / 3

=0,65m

=65cm

Panjang=7x

=7 x 0,65m

=4,55m

Lebar=4x

=4 x 0,65m

=2,6m

Luas dinding=panjang x lebar
