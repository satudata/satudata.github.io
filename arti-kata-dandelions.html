10. Change each sentence from the active to the passive voice .Simple Present and Past Tense Verbs. 1. Sarah grows dandelions in her garden. 2. The dog bit the children when they tried to tease it.​


Jawaban:

1. Dandelions are grown in Sarah's garden.

2. The children were bitten by the dog when they tried to tease it.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat pertama menggunakan bentuk simple present tense aktif yaitu 'Sarah grows dandelions in her garden'. Untuk mengubahnya ke bentuk passive voice, dapat digunakan rumus 'am/is/are + past participle'. Maka, kalimat tersebut menjadi 'Dandelions are grown in Sarah's garden'. Sedangkan, kalimat kedua menggunakan bentuk simple past tense aktif yaitu 'The dog bit the children when they tried to tease it'. Untuk mengubahnya ke bentuk passive voice, dapat digunakan rumus 'was/were + past participle'. Maka, kalimat tersebut menjadi 'The children were bitten by the dog when they tried to tease it'.
