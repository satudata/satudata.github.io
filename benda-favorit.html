20. Tuliskan cerita tentang benda favoritmu pakai bahasa Inggris


Explanation:


Descriptive text is a kind of text which is used to describe a specific person, thing, or place.


Function: The function of the descriptive text is to explain about a specific person, thing, or place to the listeners or the readers so they can understand and imagine it although they never see it before.


The structure of descriptive text:

- Definition/classification, this part contains a general description of what we want to tell to the listeners or the readers.

- Detail description, this part contains further explanation regarding the topic that has been introduced in the previous part.


Answer:

Here's a descriptive text about favorite thing.


My mobile phone

My favorite thing is my mobile phone. Its brand is Iphone. Its type is Iphone 7s. I love my mobile phone because it's not so big so i can bring it everywhere i go. Its features are also great. I can do many things by using it, such as taking a picture, playing games, and watching. It also helps me to communicate with my friends and my family. If i don't know something i can use it to find some informations on the internet. It's very useful for my daily life.
