10. The length of the right angle are (x+1) cm (4x) cm and (4x+1) cm . Calculate the value of x​


since 4x + 1 > 4x and 4x + 1 > x + 1 for x > 0, then by Pythagorean formula

(4x)² + (x + 1)² = (4x + 1)²

16x² + x² + 2x + 1 = 16x² + 8x + 1

x² - 6x = 0

x = 6
