4. Contoh kalimat Because , Because Of , dan Due to masing-masing 5 dan artinya


Answer: 

The word 'because', 'because of' and 'due to' are the clause of reason. The use of those three connectors are slightly different. 
'Because' is followed by phrase or sentence.
'Because of' and 'due to' are followed by noun or gerund. 

'Because'
1. The car is broken because the engine has been old. 
(mobilnya rusak karena mesinnya sudah tua)
2. He played well because he was in his best condition. 
(Dia bermain dengan baik karena dia dalam kondisi terbaik)
3. The book is gone because someone stole it. 
(Buku itu hilang karena ada yang mencurinya)
4. I can't swim because I'm afraid of water. 
 (Saya tidak bisa berenang karena saya takut air.)
5. The teacher will delay the test because there is an accident. 
 (Guru akan menunda tesnya karena ada kecelakaan)

Because of 
1. The match is delayed because of the weather. 
(pertandingan ditunda disebabkan oleh cuaca)
2. The boss holds the meeting soon because of the emergency situation.
(Sang boss mengadakan pertemuan segera karena situasi darurat)
3. I have to come early because of the meeting.
(saya harus datang lebih awal karena ada rapat)
4. The girl is sad because of losing her friend. 
(Gadis itu sedih karena kehilangan temannya)
5. He succeeded in finishing the project because of his good plan. 
(Dia berhasil menyelesaikan proyeknya karena perencanaan baiknya)

Due to 
1. They respect him due to his achievements.
(mereka menghormatinya karena prestasinya)
2. I salute him due to his patience.
(saya menganguminya karena kesabarannya)
3. The team lost the game due to his goalie's blunder. 
(tim itu kalah dalam pertandingan karena kesalahan kipernya)
4. He was lucky to be with Tanya due to his wealth.
(Dia beruntung bisa bersama Tanya karena kekayaannya)
5. The man was killed due to the accident. 
(seorang pria terbunuh karena kecelakaan)

Kelas: SMP
Mapel: English
Kategori: clause of reason

7. materi tentang because of, due to, thanks to. Beserta penjelasan dan contohnya


1. Dalam menggunakan kata ‘because’ kita harus melanjutkannya dengan sebuah klausa atau sebuah kalimat sempurna. Dimana kalimat ini terdiri dari subjek dan predikat.
a. … because clause, main clause
b. … main clause + because clause
*Keterangan: Kalau because nya diawal kalimat, jangan lupa untuk memberikan tanda koma
Contoh kata "because" :
• It was difficult to deliver a message because the connection was lost (Sulit untuk mengirim pesan karena koneksinya hilang)
• Because there was a demonstration on the road, Mr. Andrew arrived late (Karena ada demonstrasi di jalan, Mr. Andrew sampai terlambat)
2. Sedangkan dalam menggunakan kata ‘because of’ kita tidak membutuhkan kalimat utuh, namun kita cukup melanjutkannya dengan objeknya saja. Objek ini dapat berupa; noun (kata benda), pronoun (kata ganti), atau verb-ing
Rumus penggunaan kata "because of"
(… because of + noun(phrase) / pronoun / v-ing)
Contoh kata "because of" :
• The productivity of coffee in West Lampung decline because of the earthquake (Produktivitas kopi di Lampung Barat mengalami penurunan karena gempa bumi)
• The students come late because of the rain (Para siswa datang terlambat karena hujan)
3. "Thanks To" dipakai untuk menjelaskan bahwa sesuatu terjadi sebagai hasil dari sesuatu yang baik/positif
Contoh kata "thanks to" :
• Thanks to his hard work, he is now a very successful businessman (Berkat kerja kerasnya, sekarang menjadi wirausahawan sukses)

9. berikan 5 contoh because of, Due to, thanks to. masing" 5 contoh


Kata sambung because of, dan due to memiliki arti karena, Sementara thanks to memiliki arti berkat. Dari segi struktur bahasa ketiga jenis kata sambung ini memiliki kesamaan dalam pola kalimatnya yaitu sama-sama diikut frasa.

Pembahasan

BECAUSE OF  

Kata Because of merupakan kata sambung  yang dapat menyatakan alasan dan juga sebab akibat. Phrase ini diikuti oleh diikuti object yang berupa: noun (phrase), pronoun, atau verb-ing. Jika di lihat dari segi fungsi kata dalam sebuah kalimat, maka because of tergolong kata yang berfungsi kata sifat, kata benda, ataupun kata kerja.

Rumusnya:

BECAUSE OF + NOUN (PHRASE), PRONOUN, ATAU VERB-ING.

Contoh:

1. The man works so hard because of his family.  (Lelaki itu bekerja begitu keras karena keluarganya.)

2. The soldiers have to leave their family because of the duty.

(Para tentara harus meninggalkan keluarganya karena tugas.)

3. Because of your help, I can finish my assignment on time.

(Karena bantuanmu, Saya dapat menyelesaikan tugas ku tepat waktu.).  

4. My little brother cries so hard because of being left by my mother.

(Adik kecilku menangis sangat keras karena ditinggal oleh ibu.)

5. Because of feeling tired, She goes straight to bed.

(Karena lelah, dia langsung tidur.)

6. I cannot come to your party due to rain.

(Saya tidak bisa datang ke pestamu karena hujan).

DUE TO

Due to hampir sama dengan penggunaan because of karena due to digunakan untuk menyatakan sebab akibat dan juga menyatakan alasan seseorang melakukan sesuatu. Due to adalah kata sifat yang berfungsi untuk menjelaskan benda.

Rumusnya:

DUE TO+NOUN(PHRASE)

Contohnya:

1. The young man was lost his foot, due to car accident.

(Lelaki muda itu kehilangan kakinya, karena kecelakaan mobil)

2. I couldn’t come to your party last night due to rain.

(Aku tidak datang ke pestamu kemarin karena hujan)

3. Due to the lack of water, my mango tree doesn’t grow well.

(Karena kekurangan air, pohon manggaku tidak tumbuh dengan baik)

4. Due to a lot of job, my father comes home late tonight.

(Karena tugas yang menumpuk, ayahku pulang terlambat malam ini.)

5. The old man was lost his foot, due to car accident.

(Lelaki tua itu kehilangan kakinya, karena kecelakaan mobil.)

6. Due to the lack of water, my flowers don’t grow well.

(Karena kekurangan air, bunga-bungaku tidak tumbuh dengan baik.)

THANKS TO

Thanks to memiliki arti berkat. Thanks to digunakan menyatakan  sesuatu yang positif kepada seseorang. Lebih simple nya, ungkapan ini merupakan ungkapan rasa syukur atas sesuatu yang disebabkan oleh seseorang ataupun disebabkan oleh kondisi yang baik.  

Rumusnya:

THANKS TO + NOUN (PHRASE)

Contohnya:

1. Thanks to the untiring support of my wife, I’ve gotten back on my feet

(Berkat dukungan yang tak kenal lelah dari istriku, aku sudah kembali berdiri)

2. Thanks to his work hard, he is now very successful busseniss man.(Berkat kerja kerasnya,dia kin menjadi pembisnis paling sukses.)

3. Thanks to Fayyadh, i could be called as a mother. (Berkat Fayyadh, aku bisa disebut sebagai seorang ibu.)

4. Thanks to God, I could be life in this world. (Berkat Tuhan, Aku bisa hidup di dunia ini.)

5. Thanks to my sister, I can reach my dream. (Berkat kakakku, aku bisa menggapai mimpiku.)

PEMBAHASAN:

Pengertian Cause and Effect

Cause adalah:

The reason or motive, for an action Why something happen Happen first

Effect adalah:

Result or conclusion of an action What happened Happens due to a cause

Jadi cause and effect biasa juga disebut dengan kalimat sebab-akibat atau kalimat kausalitas adalah kalimat yang dibangun dengan melibatkan tindakan yang membuat sesuatu terjadi dan hasil dari tindakan tersebut. Paling gampang ditandai dengan kata hubung yang menandakan sebab akibat.

Signal words of cause

Ketika kita bicara tentang cause maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

Because  the reason for  On account of  Bring about  Give rise to  Created by  Contributed to  Led to  Due to  For this reason  Unless

Signal words of Effect

Ketika kita bicara tentang effect maka kata- kata yang dapat kita gunakan adalah sebagai berikut:

As a result  Then  Hence  For this reason Outcome  So  Consequently  Finally  Therefore  In order to

The sentence structure of cause and effect

Due to/Because of/owing to/Thanks to + noun Because/Since/As/For/As a result of + subject + verb

17. perbedaan because of,due to,thanks to


DUE TO merupakan ADJECTIVE (kata sifat) yang berfungsi menjelaskan benda, sesuai dengan penggunaan kata sifat menurut grammar yang benar.

Sedangkan BECAUSE OF merupakan ADVERB (kata keterangan) yang sudah tentu untuk menjelaskan verb, adjective dan noun sesuai dengan aturan grammar.

THANKS TO
Berbeda dengan dua preposisi sebelumnya, thanks to dipakai untuk menjelaskan bahwa sesuatu terjadi sebagai hasil dari sesuatu yang baik/positif. Maka, thanks to tidak bisa menggantikan because of/owing to dalam kalimat pertama di atas. Tetapi, frasa ini bisa menggantikan dua frasa lain dalam kalimat kedua. Frasa ini lebih tepat diartikan berkat dalam pengertian karena.

22. dialog bahasa inggris lengkap dengan thanks to, because of dan due to


Here is the dialogue with the connectors such as thanks to, because of and due to as mentioned above:

Vitor: What a great season for English football this year, ain't it?

Seamus: Aye it is, thanks to the performance our elite clubs though, English football is making a history. This is the first time that all finalists of Champions League and Europa League in one season come from one country.

Vitor: Really?

Seamus: True, mate. Even Spain never does it.

Vitor: So, why do you think they manage to do so?

Seamus: I suppose those English finalists, Liverpool, Tottenham, Arsenal and Chelsea gain their success because of their managers.

Vitor: Hmm, I couldn't agree more, but is it really the only thing which triggers such achievement?

Seamus: I believe so. In UCL, Klopp and Pocchettino are undeniably better managers than Valverde of Barcelona and Erik Ten Hag of Ajax. Due to their excellent tactics, Liverpool and Tottenham could really turn over their disadvantage in the first leg into a great victory.

Vitor: I see your point. Not to mention that the EPL always provide a regularly competitive matches in almost every week, literally. They get used to it. What about in Europa League?

Seamus: The way I see it, it's the experience that makes the difference. Unai Emery holds the record as the most manager ever win Europa League in history with Sevilla before. So, it's Arsenal's advantage. Meanwhile, Maurizio Sarri is more experienced in dealing with the rivals in this competition for non elite teams. He worked for Napoli before and did fine. Whereas, Chelsea has better squad than his previous team. So, ideally he should do better as well.

Pembahasan:

Those connectors mentioned above are useful to make complex sentences with their own function.

'Thanks to' is normally stated to express your gratitude to other parties. The pattern is 'Thanks to + noun, clause' .The example is

        'Thanks to your contribution, I was able to get this far.'

        'Thanks to the new regulation, smoking is finally banned.'

'Because of' and 'Due to' is commonly used to state reason on something. The pattern is 'S + V + O + complement because of + noun'   and 'Due to + noun, clause'. The example are as follows                            

        'They are late because of getting stuck in a long traffic jam.'

        'Due to her persistence, he finally gave in.'

In English, connectors like 'because of' and 'due to' are to state cause and effect. Meanwhile, 'thanks to' is used for stating gratitude. All of them are followed by noun or phrase. Here's the example of them in a sentence:
- She failed the test because of her own fault. 
- He had to be sent off due to his horrendous tackle.
- Thanks to your advice. Now I can get it done fast.

What is the social fungsion of due to, because of and thanks to3). Make 3 sentences using " due to, "3 sentences for " because of "3 sentences for " thanks to"​


Penjelasan:

1. What do you know about

They are words used to express a reason in a different ways, based by their grammar.

a. Due to = dikarenakan oleh

b. Because of = karena

c. Thanks to = Terima kasih kepada / karena mu

2. What is the social function of :

a. Due to = dikarenakan oleh

• Berbentuk adjektiva / kata sifat

• Frasa ini digunakan untuk memodifikasi kata benda. Dengan kata lain, karena digunakan untuk menyajikan alasan untuk kata benda

• selalu digunakan setelah form "to be" (is, am, are, was, were, etc)

Ex : The traffic jam was due to a terrible accident at the intersection.

.

Kata traffic jam dijelaskan dengan due to

b. Because of = Karena

• Frasa ini digunakan untuk memodifikasi tindakan. Dengan kata lain, karena digunakan untuk menyajikan alasan suatu tindakan

• tidak digunakan setelah form "to be"

Ex : The traffic jam happened because of a terrible accident at the intersection.

.

Kata happened dijelaskan dengan because of

c. Thanks to = Terima kasih kepada / karena mu

• Digunakan untuk ungkapan kepada sesuatu/seseorang yang bertanggung jawab terhadap suatu hal (bisa jadi ungkapan positif / negatif)

Ex :

(+) My face is a lot better, thanks to the skincare product

(-) I lost all of my money, thanks to the robber
