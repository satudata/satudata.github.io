26. deskribsi tentang busana butik


Batik is a pictorial fabric that is specially made by writing or putting the night on the cloth,then processing is processed in a certain way that has a specificity.

Batik is a craft that has high artistic value and has been a part of Indonesian culture (especially Java) since a long time.

The word batik comes from the Javanese language, which is "tick" meaning point / matik (verb, make a point) which later developed into the term "batik".

The art of coloring the cloth with the coloring blocking technique using the night is one of the ancient art forms.
batik is believed to have existed since the time of Majapahit, and became very popular by the end of the XVIII century or early nineteenth century. Batik produced is all batik tulis until the beginning of XX century and new batik caps known after World War I or about the 1920s.
