11. The text is written to Congratulate. (Jawaban B) ✔

➡ Teks tersebut ditulis untuk memberi ucapan selamat.

Keterangan: A. Share happiness. = Berbagi kebahagiaan B. Congratulate. = SelamatC. Appreciate = mengapresiasi D. Hope. = harapan.

Jadi, opsi B tepat karena dalam teks terteraj jelas "Congratulations" yang artinya "selamat."

12. From the text we know that Hardy Has worked hard to achieve highest score. (Jawaban C) ✔

➡ Dari teks kita tahu bahwa Hardy telah berusaha keras untuk meraih nilai tertinggi.

Keterangan: A. A teacher in a district where he lives.= Seorang guruB. Is the best teacher in his school. = Guru terbaik di sekolahnya.C. Has worked hard to achieve highest score. = telah berusaha keras untuk meraih nilai tertinggi.D. Sanias' friend in his school.= Teman sania di sekolahnya.

Jadi, opsi C tepat karena berdasarkan teks pada kalimat kedua "you study hard" yang artinya belajar dengan giat.

13. We are so exultant at your achievement. The word 'exultant' has the closest meaning to happy. (Jawaban C) ✔

➡ Kata exultant sama artinya dengan happy.

Keterangan: A. Sad = SedihB. Proud = BanggaC. Happy = BahagiaD. Jealous = Cemburu

Jadi, opsi C tepat karena exultant berarti gembira yang sama artinya dengan happy.
