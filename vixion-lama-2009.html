18. 2009/2010 : 1/2011 =2009 + c = tentukan nilai c....

(2009 x 2011)/2010 = 2009 + c
c = 2010/2011[tex] \frac{2009}{2010}: \frac{1}{2011} =2009+c \\ \\ \frac{2009}{2010}.\frac{2011}{1}=2009+c \\ \\ \frac{2009(2010+1)}{2010}=2009+c \\ \\ \frac{2009.2010+2009.1)}{2010}=2009+c \\ \\ \frac{2009.2010}{2010}+\frac{2009}{2010}=2009+c \\ \\ 2009+\frac{2009}{2010}=2009+c \\ \\ c=\frac{2009}{2010}[/tex]
