14. Teks prosedur tema Pepes ikan


Pepes is one of the traditional dishes in Indonesia. It is made with banana leaves as the food wrappings and you can use various types of animal protein, such as fish; chicken; tofu and even vegetables. Here is how you make delicious pepes with tilapia fish.

"PEPES IKAN NILA" RECIPE

Ingredients:

Main:

1 kilogram of Tilapia fillets

1 tbsp. of lime juice

Banana leaves for wrapping

Ground spices:

10 cloves of shallots

6 cloves of garlics

3 candlenuts

2 tsp turmeric

1 tsp coriander

5 red chilies

5 bird's eye chilies

2 kaffir lime leaves

Salt to taste

Sugar to taste

Complementary:

2 tomatoes, cut into pieces

2 basil leaves

Bird's eye chilies to taste

Directions:

Clean the tilapia fish, then add the lime juice. Set aside for 10 minutes then rinse again.Grind all the ground spices: shallots, garlics, candlenuts, turmeric, coriander, red chilies, bird's eye chilies, kaffir lime leaves, salt and sugar.Take a banana leaf, put the fish and the ground spices on the leaf, add chopped tomatoes, basil leaves and whole bird's eye chili according to taste. Roll it up and then pin the ends with a stick.Steam the Pepes for 45 minutes until thoroughly cooked.After that, briefly grill the pepes on a pan so that the flavor is more fragrant.Pepes tilapia is ready to be served.

16. Harga dua buah paha ayam goreng dan satu pepes ikan adalah Rp42.000,00. Jika harga tiga paha ayam gorenga dan dua pepes ikan adalah Rp69.000,00, maka harga satu paha ayam goreng dan satu pepes ikan adalah


Jawab: Rp27.000

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Misal paha ayam = x dan pepes ikan = y

2x + y = 42.000

3x + 2y = 69.000

Maka dengan prinsip eliminasi kita dapat mengkalkulasi :

3x + 2y - (2x + y) = 69.000 - 42.000

x + y = 27.000

Harga 1 paha ayam dan 1 pepes ikan adalah Rp27.000
