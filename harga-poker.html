23. dalam permainan poker, setiap pemain mendapatkan lima kartu. hitung peluang bahwa salah seorang pemain mendapatkan a. 3 ace b. 4 hati dan 2 klaver


The formula of combination We use in this brainly writing space is
C(n,r) = n!/[r!(n-r)!]

Solutiion:

(Number a.)
There are C(52,5) = 2,598,960 ways of choosing 5 cards. There are C(4,3) = 4 ways to select the 3 aces. So, there are C(49,2) = 49×24 = 1,176 ways to select the remaining cards. Thus there are a total of 4×1,176 ways to select 5 cards such that 3 of them are aces,
and the probability is (4×1176)/2,598,960 = 2/1105 =0.0018099548

Number b.)
There are C(1,1) = 1 way to choose the heart no.4 , there are C(1,1) = 1 way to choose clover no.2 and C(50,3) = 19600. So there are 1×19600 = 19600 ways to choose 5 cards such that the heart no.4 and the clover no.2 and the other cards . The probability is 19600/2,598,960
= 0.0075414781
