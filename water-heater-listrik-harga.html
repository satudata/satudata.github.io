11. A heater used to heat 4 kg of water at 25°C until boiling for 10 minutes. If the specific heat of water is 4200 J/kg°C. Calculate the power of heater!​


Penjelasan:

Dik :

- M : 4 Kg

- Suhu : 25°C

- T : 10 Menit = 600 Sekon

- C : 4.200 J/kg°C

Dit : P?

Jawab :

(PAKI RUMUS KETERKAITAN KALOR DAN DAYA)

P×T = M×C×Suhu

P×600 = 4×4.200×25

P×600 = 420.000

P = 420.000/600

P = 700 Watts.

Jadi,daya pemanas air tersebut adalah 700 Watts.
