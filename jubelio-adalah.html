25. Ini Kubelikan Hp Nak Tapi Di Tentukan Dulu tentukan HP dari |5x-10|=-5​


`Persamaan nilai mutlak

|5x - 10| = -5

Jika x ≥ 0

|5x - 10| = -5

5x - 10 = -5

5x = -5 + 10

5x = 5

x = 5/5

x = 1

Jika x < 0

|5x - 10| = -5

-(5x - 10) = -5

-5x + 10 = -5

-5x = -5 - 10

-5x = -15

x = -15/-5

x = 3

HP{x|x = 1 or x = 3, x € R}

Jawab:

{ 1 , 3}

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

tentukan HP dari |5x-10|=-5​

=========================================

| 5x - 10 | = - 5

Negatiff

5x  - 10 = 5

5x = 5 + 10

5x = 15

x = 3

Positif

5x - 10 = - 5

5x = 5
