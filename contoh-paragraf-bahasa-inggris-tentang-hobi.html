13. menceritakan hobi dalam bahasa inggris 4 paragraf/100kata


Menceritakan hobi dalam Bahasa Inggris:

I want to tell you about my hobbies. I love to read books during my spare time. I usually read books on the weekend, after eating my lunch. I love to read fiction and non-fiction books. One of the best non-fiction book that I've ever read is Charlie and the Chocolate Factory by Roald Dahl. This book also has been filmed in 2005!

I love drawing, eventhough I am not good at it. Drawing and colouring can help me to express my emotion and release negative emotion. By seeing and having fun with colours, we also practice our creativity. Nowadays, you can draw and colour in your phone/ tablet, not only on papers!

Listening to music also can boost my mood! When I was bored, I listen to pop music. There are so many song writers around the world, and I really admire them! One of the coolest song writer for me is Taylor Swift. She was born in America. I also like to hear K-Pop and Indonesian music.

Good hobbies will make us a better and more creative person! Don't hesitate to try and pursue new hobbies, especially during Covid-19 pandemic. You can explore and do many things even when staying at home!
