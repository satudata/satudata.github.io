A. 3 8/40

B. 3 25/40

C. 3 33/40

D. 4 33/40

2 1/5 + 1 5/8 = 2 8/40 + 1 25/40 = 3 33/40

A. 1/2 + 1/3

B. 1 1/3 + 1/3

C. 1/4 + 1 1/3

D. 1 1/3 + 1/2

Cara mengerjakan:

1/2 + 1/3 = 3/6 + 2/6 = 5/61 1/3 + 1/3 = 1 2/31/4 + 1 1/3 = 3/12 + 1 4/12 = 1 7/121 1/3 + 1/2 = 1 2/6 + 3/6 = 1 5/6

A. 2 1/3

B. 2 1/6

C. 1 11/12

D. 5/6

Cara mengerjakan:2 2/3 – 1 5/6 = 2 4/6 – 1 5/6 = 1 10/6 – 1 5/6 = 5/6

A. 3 3/4

B. 3 1/2

C. 3 1/4

D. 2 3/4

Cara mengerjakan:4 3/4 – 1 1/2 = 4 3/4 – 1 2/4 = 3 1/4

A. 3 7/8

B. 3 1/8

C. 2 7/8

D. 2 1/8

8. Hasil pengurangan yang hasilnya 5/8 adalah ….

A. 1 1/2 – 3/8

B. 1 1/8 – 5/8

C. 1 1/8 – 1/2

D. 1 1/3 – 1/2

Cara mengerjakan:1 1/2 – 3/8 = 1 4/8 – 3/8 = 1 1/81 1/8 – 5/8 = 9/8 – 5/8 = 4/8 = 1/21 1/8 – 1/2 = 1 1/8 – 4/8 = 9/8 – 4/8 = 5/81 1/3 – 1/2 = 1 2/6 – 3/6 = 8/6 – 3/6 = 5/6
