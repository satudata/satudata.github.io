Jaguar


Jaguar is the largest cat in the Americas. They have a muscular body, a strong jaw, and a broad head. Their entire body is covered by yellow and brown hair.

They are carnivores and are one of the most feared predators by herbivorous animals. They are also have a value to human culture. These animals often have a central role in the story, songs, and prayers of the American people. However, today Jaguar has completely disappeared from America. They become rare animals and are prohibited to be hunted.

As carnivores, they eat anything that can be caught. Deer, crocodiles, snakes, turtles, eggs, frogs, fish, and monkeys are the variation of the animals they catch.

In the worldwide, jaguar populations are so little. Currently they are probably only about 15,000. Some regions in the Americas continue to provide protection for them. Various conservations are done in the area of ​​Sonora and Mexico in order that they do not die out completely. If a long time ago you can see jaguars roamed in Argentina up to the Grand Canyon, now it seems that you hardly find them. They have been completely destroyed and the population in Sonora is the last hope for their population.

They are animals that can live anywhere. You can see the jaguar in the rain forests, swamps, grasslands, deciduous forests, and mountainous areas. Usually, they live alone, except in the mating season. They are animals that spend a large part of their time on the ground. Sometimes, they can climb trees to catch the prey.

As predators that have strong jaws and sharp teeth, they can crush the skull with just one bite. Then, they are different from other cats. They love to swim and do not hesitate if they need to jump into the water for swimming and catching the prey.

21. Pak Yaris melakukan perjalanan dari Yogyakarta menuju Bali pada pukul 07.00 Waktu Indonesia Barat dengan lama perjalanan 1 jam Pak Yaris tiba ditempat tujuan pada pukul


Mapel : IPS
Materi : Perbedaan Waktu
Kelas : 6

Selisih WIB dan WITA adalah 1 jam, berarti ;

= Waktu awal + Waktu perjalanan
= 08.00 + Waktu selisih WITA
= 09.00 WITA

Jadi, Pak Yaris akan tiba di tujuan pada jam 09.00 WITA.

27. Pak Yaris melakukan perjalanan dari Yogyakarta menuju Bali pada pukul 07.00 WIB dengan lama perjalanan satu jam. Pak Yaris tiba di tempat tujuan pada pukul .... *A.08.00 WITAB.09.00 WITAC.11.00 WITAD.10.00 WITA​


Jawaban:

B. 09.00 WITA

Penjelasan:

Karena Indonesia di belah menjadi 3 zona waktu, setiap zona waktu berbeda 1 jam.

WIB - WITA = 1 jam.

WITA - WIT = 1 jam.

WIB - WIT = 2 jam.

Pak Yaris melakukan perjalanan dari jam 7 dan tiba jam 8, tetapi karena Yogyakarta di zona waktu WIB, dan Bali di zona waktu WITA, maka maju 1 jam.

B. 09.00 WITA

Karena perbedaan WIB-WITA hanya satu jam

Jadi saat pak yaris sudah sampai bali sudah jam 08.00 WIB atau Jam 09.00 WITA
