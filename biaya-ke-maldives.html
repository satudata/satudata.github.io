13. Levi mempunyai uang Rp150.000.000 mau wisata ke maldives Pada saat itu kurs beli Rp12.000 dan kurs jual Rp12.500 Kembali dari Maldives masih memiliki $150 kurs beli Rp 13.000 dan kurs jual Rp 13.500 Hitunglah sisa uangnya! pls jan asal ya


Jumlah uang Leni sebelum berangkat ke Maldives Rp 150 jt. Untuk menjadikannya mata uang asing, maka yg berlaku yaitu kurs jual.
$ = nilai Rp / kurs jual
= 150 jt / 12.500
=$12.000

Jumlah uang yg dihabiskan = $12.000 - $150
= $11.850

Sisa uang setelah kembali dari Maldives yaitu $150
Untuk menukarkannya dengan mata uang rupiah maka yg berlaku adalah Kurs beli. Berikut perhitungannya:
Rp = nilai mata uang asing × kurs beli
=$150 × 13.000
= Rp 1.950.000,00

Jadi, sisa uang Levi setelah kembali berwisata dari Maldives sebesar Rp 1.950.000,00

fyi = sebenarnya bisa langsung hitung sisa uangnya yg $150 (klo mau cepet).

14. simple descriptive text about maldives island


The Maldive Islands is a South Asian Island Country located in the indian Ocean. The archipelago is situated in the Arabian Sea and lies South West of Sri Lanka and India. The Maldives are formed by a number of natural atols and has a chain of 26 atolls plus atolls in the form of isolated reefs. The atolls of Maldives are very complex structures and were formally dangerous to explorers and navigators. 

In the 12th century, the Maldivan archipelago accepted Islam as a religion and consolidates a sultunate later on which developed strong commercial and cultural ties with Asia and Africa. From the 16th-century, the archipelago region came under heavy influence of the European colonial powers, with the Maldives becoming a British territory under the British colonisation of India. They gained their independance in 1986 and elected the first Majlis of the people.
