5. A. Find the meaning of the following words!1. Overslept (V) :2. Miss (V)3. Nightmare (N):4. Clutch (V)5. Huge (Adj.)​6. bounce (v)7. touchdown (n)8. approach (v)9. passenger (n)10. stare (v)


Jawaban:

Find the meaning of the following words!

1. Overslept (V): to sleep longer or later than you intended to

2. Miss (V): to fail to do or experience something, often something planned or expected, or to avoid doing or experiencing something

3. Nightmare (N): a very upsetting or frightening dream

4. Clutch (V): to take or try to take hold of something tightly, usually in fear, worry, or pain

5. Huge (Adj.)​: extremely large in size or amount

6. Bounce (v): to (cause to) move up or away after hitting a surface

7. Touchdown (n): the landing of an aircraft or of some types of spacecraft

8. Approach (v): to come near or nearer to something or someone in space, time, quality, or amount

9. Passenger (n): a person who is travelling in a vehicle but is not driving it, flying it, or working on it

10. Stare (v): to look for a long time with the eyes wide open, especially when surprised, frightened, or thinking

6. miss intan walks (elegant)............. than miss diah does​


Jawaban:

Miss Intan walks more elegantly than Miss Diah does​.

Penjelasan:

Nona Intan berjalan dengan lebih elegan dibandingkan Nona Diah.

Comparative Form

Menunjukkan perbandingan 2 benda/hal --> bahasa Indonesianya lebih.

Rumus:

Noun (subject) + verb + comparative adjective + than + noun (object).

Regular Comparative

1) Jika adjectivenya terdiri dari satu silabel --> adjective + -er (*catatan: kalau adjective satu silabel tersusun dari konsonan + vokal (1) + konsonan, maka konsonan akhir harus didobel). Misal:

tall --> taller big --> bigger

2) Jika adjectivenya terdiri dari dua silabel --> ada 2 cara, bisa 1) adjective + -er atau 2) more + adjective. Dua-duanya bisa benar, tergantung mana yang lebih umum dipakai. Kalau mau aman pakai cara (2). Misal:

angry --> angrier careful --> more careful

3) Jika adjectivenya terdiri dari 3 atau lebih silabel --> more + adjective. Misal:

important --> more important beautiful --> more beautiful

Irregular Comparative

Tidak ada polanya --> harus dihafalkan. Misal:

good --> better bad --> worse far --> further/farther
