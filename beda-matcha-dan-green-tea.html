23. Abel membeli 3 iced green tea dan 4 iced milk tea seharga Rp120.000,-, sementara Brigitta membeli 3 iced milk tea dan 2 iced coffee seharga Rp105.000,-. Jika kemudian diketahui bahwa Cindy membeli 3 iced green tea dan 1 iced coffee seharga Rp90.000,-, berapa yang harus dibayar jika Daniel membeli 4 iced green tea, 4 iced milk tea dan 3 iced coffee? a. Rp. 200.000 b. Rp. 210.000 c. Rp. 220.000 d. Rp. 230.000


Jawaban:

D. 230.000

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Misal :

Green tea = x

Milk tea = y

Coffe = z

3x + 4y = Rp120.000 (pers 1)

3y + 2z = Rp105.000 (pers 2)

3x + z = Rp90.000 (pers 3)

Eliminasi Z pada pers 2 dan 3

3y + 2z = 105.000 (dikali 1) = 3y + 2z = 105.000

3x + z = 90.000 (dikali 2) = 6x + 2z = 180.000

3y + 2z = 105.000

6x + 2z = 180.000

(Dikurangi) = 3y - 6x = -75.000 (pers 4)

Eliminasi x pada pers 1 dan 4

3x + 4y = 120.000 (dikali 2) = 6x + 8y = 240.000

3y - 6x = -75.000 (dikali 1) = 3y - 6x = -75.000

6x + 8y = 240.000

3y - 6x = -75.000

(Ditambah) = 11y = 165.000

y = 15.000

Substitusikan y = 15.000 ke pers 1

3x + 4(15.000) = Rp120.000

3x = 120.000 - 60.000

3x = 60.000

x = 20.000

Substitusikan x = 20.000 ke pers 3

3(20.000) + z = Rp90.000

z = 90.000 - 60.000

z = 30.000

Harga green tea = 20.000

Harga milk tea = 15.000

Harga coffe = 30.000

Daniel membeli 4 iced green tea, 4 iced milk tea dan 3 ice coffe = 4(20.000) + 4(15.000) + 3(30.000) = 80.000 + 60.000 + 90.000 = 230.000
