How to Save Videos from YouTube to the Gallery Without Applications

There are many ways to download videos from YouTube to your device, one of which uses the Free YouTube Video Downloader service from Savefrom.net. This web-based service provides YouTube video converter with various formats.

Open the YouTube application, then find the video that the user wants to download

If the video has been found, click the dot icon in the top right corner of the video

Tap "Share", then select copy link

After the video URL is copied, open the smartphone browser and type savefrom.net

Paste the URL that has been copied into the search box

Wait for the video thumbnail to appear
