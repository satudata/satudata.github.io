7. sebutkan macam-macam formulir dalam bahasa Inggris dan pengertian nya​


Jawaban:

1. Purchase form

Purchase form is also known as purchase order (PO) form. Purchase form is a commercial document which is made by a buyer to a seller and used as a request to purchase products or services. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products or service purchased.

2. Order form

Order form is a commercial document which is filled in by customers and used to order goods from a company. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products ordered.

3. Withdrawal slip

Withdrawal slip is a piece of paper which is filled in by a client before withdrawing cash from an account. The slip is generally used in a bank or building society. The slip is used as a proof of withdrawal. This slip contains the date of withdrawal, the client's name, the client's account number, the client's signature, and the amount of withdrawal.

4. Deposit slip

Deposit slip is a piece of paper which is filled in by a client before depositing cash into an account. The slip is generally used in a bank and building society. The slip is used as a proof of depositing money. This slip contains the date of depositing, the client's name, the client's account number, the amount of money deposited, and the client's signature.

5. Reservation form

Reservation form is a form which is filled in when booking or reserving something. it can be a hotel room, entertainment tickets, transportation, etc. The form is used to book or reserve it to be used later. This form contains the client's information, a number of things or places booked, and the date and time of when it's used. Some reservation forms requires down payment as a reservation deal.

6. Medical form

Medical form is a form which is filled in by a patient to a doctor. The form is used for applying medical check up and as a reference for the doctor to take what kind of treatment which should be taken for the patient. This form contains the patient's personal health information, such as his/her illness, allergies, currently taken medicines, family health history, etc.

14. jelaskan pengertian prezent continous tense dan contoh kalimat pasive nya dalam bahasa inggris...


•present continuous tense adalah
Kalimat untuk menyatakan suatu pekerjaan/kegiatan yg sedang dilakukan pada saat itu.
Contoh:
(+)I'm reading the book in libary
(-)I am not reading the book in libary
(?)are you reading the book in libarypresent continuous adalah tenses yang menerangkan suatu perbuatan yang sedang berlangsung pada waktu sekarang. Contoh kalimat passif nya.

Rumus aktif : S + To be (am/are/is) + V-ing + O
Rumus pasif : S + To be (am/are/is) + being + V3 + by

NOTE :
Subjek di kalimat aktif menjadi objek dikalimat pasif, begitu sebaliknya.

contoh :
Active : She is cooking fried rice.
Passive : Fried rice is being cooked by her.

20. Apa pengertian teks prosedur dalam bahasa inggris. Dan contoh pertanyaan nya dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Procedure Text

Penjelasan:

Procedure text is a text that is designed to describe how

something is achieved through a sequence of actions or steps

Example:

How to Clean an LCD Screen

1. Shake up the detergent and put it 15 — 20 cm away from the LCD screen.

2. Spray on the LCD screen surface directly.

3. Cleanse gently using the fabric cleanser or brush.

You can also do the following steps:

1. Spray the detergent on the fabric cleanser.

2. Clean the LCD screen surface to make it as bright as a new one.

Contoh Pertanyaan :

↓ ↓ ↓

1. What should you do to detergent first?

A. Spray it

B. Clean it

C. Put it away

D. Shake it up

2. Before cleaning the screen surface, we spray the detergent on the screen surface directly or spray it on ….

A. Our fingers

B. The paper

C. The fabric cleanser

D. The brush

3. "Cleanse gently with the fabric cleanser or brush" (step 3). What does the underlined word mean?

A. Carefully

B. Directly

C. Harshly

D. Cleanly

22. worker dalam bahasa inggris pengertian nya


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : VOCABULARY

KELAS : 10

KODE SOAL : 10.5

KODE KATEGORISSI : 10.5.3


PEMBAHASAN

================

Arti dari "worker" adalah...


WORK (verb) = Kerja

WORKER (noun) = Pekerja


Jika di definisikan dalam bahasa inggris, maka WORKER is a person who does a specified type of work or who works in a specified way.

menurut oxford dictionaries, definisi worker adalah:

1. A person who does a specified type of work or who works in a specified way (Seseorang yang melakukan jenis pekerjaan tertentu atau yang bekerja dengan cara tertentu)

2. A person who achieves a specified thing (Seseorang yang mencapai hal tertentu)

3. A neuter or undeveloped female bee, wasp, ant, or other social insect, large numbers of which do the basic work of the colony. (Lebah betina yang netral atau tidak berkembang, tawon, semut, atau serangga sosial lainnya, sebagian besar melakukan pekerjaan dasar koloni.)

Intinya, Worker artinya Pekerja.
