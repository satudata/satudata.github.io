9. Ulfa menjual jaket yang baru dibelinya karena kekecilan kepada temannya dengan harga Rp500.000. Jika Ulfa mendapatkan untung 25%, maka harga beli jaket tersebut adalah...Tlg Kakak...​


Mapel : Matematika

Kelas : 7

Materi : Aritmatika sosial

Kode kategorisasi : 7.2.7

Ulfa menjual jaket yang baru dibelinya karena kekecilan kepada temannya dengan harga Rp500.000.

Jika Ulfa mendapatkan untung 25%, maka harga beli jaket tersebut adalah Rp 400.000

Pembahasan

Karena harga beli nominalnya selalu 100%, maka rumusnya

Harga beli = 100/ 100 + %untung × HJ

Penyelesaian

HB = 100/100 + 25% × 500.000

HB = 100/125 × 500.000

HB = 400.000

Pembuktian

Jika Ulfa mendapat untung sebesar 25% maka,

keuntungan = 400.000 × 25%

= 400.000 × 25/100

= 100.000

Maka terbukti jika Ulfa membeli harga jaket senilai RP 400.000 karena ia menjulanya seharga Rp 500.000 utk mendapat untung 25% senilai RP 100.000

Kesimpulanmaka harga beli jaket tersebut adalah Rp 400.000

Expressions of Giving Compliments - Dialog

Andy’s New Jacket

Lusi: Hey Andy, you look fabulous today!

Andy: Really? Thanks Lusi!

Lusi: Yeah, really! And by the way that jacket that you’re wearing looks great on you. Is that new?

Andy: Yup! My mother got me for my 15th birthday last week. I have been dreaming about this jacket, and finally I got it!

Lusi: I can tell that your jacket is well made. Look at the fabric and the stitches! It must be expensive, I bet.

Andy: Well, yes it’s quite expensive that’s why I asked as a birthday gift.

Pembahasan

Compliment adalah sebuah ungkapan yang kita gunakan untuk memberikan pujian kepada orang lain.

Formula yang digunakan pada umumnya ketika membuat ungkapan compliment adalah sebagai berikut:

1. How + Adjective + Noun + is/are.

Contoh:

How beautiful flower is.How big ocean isHow careful jobs are

2. What + Adjetive + Noun.

Contoh:

What a beautiful flower.What a big houseWhat a wonderful job

3. Noun Phrase + is/look + (really) + Adjective.

Contoh:

Your hair is really beautifulThe skirt is niceHis performance is outstanding

4. Noun/Pronoun + (really) + like/love + Noun Phrase.

Contoh:

We all really love your performanceAll teachers really love your commitmentsHis father really like his test result

5. Pronoun + is + (really) + Adjective + Noun Phrase.

Contoh:

That is a really beautiful t-shirtThis is a really wonderful paintingThat’s a great looking car.
